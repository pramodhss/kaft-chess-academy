Crafting Grandmasters Since 2005

KAFT Chess Academy was founded with a singular vision — to cultivate strategic thinking, discipline, and excellence through the royal game of chess. Over nearly two decades, we have grown into a nationally recognized institution.

Our certified coaches guide students from their very first move all the way through competitive tournament play. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned player aiming for a title, KAFT has the right program for you.

  • FIDE-rated coaching staff
  • 80+ tournament championships
  • 500+ trained students
  • International tournament representation
  • Structured curriculum for all ages

Our Programs

Structured learning paths for every skill level and age group

Beginner

Foundation Course

Perfect for complete beginners aged 6 and above. Learn the rules, piece movements, and basic strategies.

  • Piece movement and rules
  • Basic tactics and checkmate patterns
  • Fun mini-tournaments
  • 6-week program
Intermediate

Competitive Training

For players with a basic understanding ready to compete in local and regional tournaments.

  • Opening theory and preparation
  • Tactical puzzles and drills
  • Endgame techniques
  • 12-week program
Advanced

Elite Mastery

Designed for rated players aiming for FIDE titles and national/international competition.

  • Advanced opening repertoire
  • Grandmaster game analysis
  • Tournament preparation
  • Ongoing coaching
